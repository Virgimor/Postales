From: Jhonnavie



To: Elmer B.

The Castle of Santa Catalina stands on one of the most spectacular sites in the province of Jaén, is a local wonder. The current visible construction is of Christian origin, being erected after the conquest of the city by Ferdinand III the Saint in 1246.

Strolling through its corridors and having a picnic in the nearby lands is an unforgettable experience. In fact, the people of Jaén traditionally meet once a year to eat next to the castle. Would you like to join the next one?
